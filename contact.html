Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any experience to start?

Not at all! Our classes are specifically designed for beginners. We'll teach you everything you need to know, starting from the very basics.

What should I wear to my first class?

Just wear comfortable workout clothes that you can move freely in. We provide all necessary equipment for your trial class.

Is the trial class really free?

Yes! Your first class is completely free with no obligation. It's our way of letting you experience our teaching style and community.

How long are the classes?

Most classes are 60 minutes long. This gives us enough time for warm-up, technique instruction, practice, and cool-down.

What ages do you teach?

We offer programs for all ages! Kids classes start at age 6, and we have adult programs for all fitness levels and ages.
